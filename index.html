About Me

Who am I?

Hello! I'm Binara Akalanka, a passionate web developer live in Kaluthara, Sri Lanka. With 1 years of experience in creating responsive and user-friendly websites, I blend technical expertise with creative design to deliver exceptional digital experiences.

My journey into web development began during my university years, where I discovered my love for coding and design. Since then, I’ve been continuously learning and building sample projects to refine my skills and explore different aspects of modern web technologies. While I haven't worked with clients just yet, I'm actively developing my portfolio and looking forward to collaborating on real-world projects soon.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring anime art, playing video games, or hiking in the beautiful mountains around Sri Lanka. I believe that these diverse interests help fuel my creativity and bring a unique perspective to my work.
